Why The Porch Project

Our community shows up.
We made it stick.

Someone asks for a plumber recommendation in the neighborhood group — and 40 neighbors show up to help within the hour. That's one of the best things about Steiner Ranch. We actually take care of each other.

The only problem? That question gets asked 47 times a year, and the answers disappear into the scroll every single time.

The Porch Project is where those answers finally live. A permanent, curated resource built from the collective knowledge of the people already in this community — so the next neighbor who needs a plumber at 7am can just find one.

"The people who care most about a community are the ones already living in it. We're just building them the front porch they've been missing."

  • Great recommendations, all in one place Your neighbors' collective knowledge is invaluable — we've just organized it so you can get what you need without wading through the noise.
  • Just moved in and not sure where to start? From the best after-school activities and sports leagues to where to actually meet people — our newcomer page has the things it takes a year to figure out on your own.
  • Never miss what's happening around you Our events page keeps everything in one spot — so you always know what's going on in and around Steiner Ranch.
  • Want to support neighbors who run businesses Our "Porch Neighbor" badge flags businesses owned by people who actually live in Steiner Ranch.

Built on Trust

We do our best
to know who's in here.

The badges mean something specific. Porch Neighbor means the owner lives in Steiner Ranch. Porch Verified means the community has spoken well of them. Down the road, there may be options for businesses to have greater visibility on the platform — but a badge can't be bought, and being here still means you belong here.

Beyond that, we rely on you. This directory is only as good as the people behind it — and that includes everyone who lives here. If a listing doesn't belong, let us know.
